Mission-Driven Stability

The Veritas Protocol was established by the Veritas Foundation to address the need for a truly decentralized and robust store-of-value within the Gnosis ecosystem. Our mission is to provide a reliable on-chain asset that can serve as a core building block for other DeFi applications, enhancing the overall security of the network.

Unlike traditional fixed-supply assets, Veritas Token (VRT) is engineered to be a self-defending instrument that actively contributes to its own stability during periods of market stress.

Unique Feature: Dynamic Stability Fee (DSF)

VRT is the first asset on Gnosis to incorporate the Dynamic Stability Fee. This mechanism allows the protocol to autonomously monitor on-chain DEX volatility. During extreme market turbulence, a microscopic, pre-defined transaction fee is activated.

Revenue from this fee is routed to the protocol's treasury, managed by the Foundation, with the sole purpose of deepening liquidity where it is needed most. During normal market conditions, the fee remains at 0%, ensuring VRT is efficient for everyday use. This makes VRT an intelligent asset that protects holders and partners like Aave by inherently mitigating risk.

Secure and Deliberate Governance

The Veritas Protocol is stewarded by the Veritas Foundation. All administrative functions, including DSF parameter adjustments and future security upgrades, are controlled by a 2-of-3 Gnosis Safe multi-signature wallet. This structure ensures that no single individual can unilaterally alter the protocol, enforcing a consensus-driven approach to any changes. This is our commitment to the security of our users and partners.
